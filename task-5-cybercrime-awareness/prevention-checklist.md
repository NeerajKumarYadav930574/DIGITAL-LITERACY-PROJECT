# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist
### Digital Literacy Guide for Students

Follow these 8 actionable steps to protect your digital identity and financial assets.

1.  **Never Share Your UPI PIN for Receiving Money:** Always remember that a PIN is only required to *send* or *deduct* money from your account. If someone asks you to enter your PIN to "receive" a payment, it is a scam.
2.  **Verify UPI "Collect Requests":** Before approving any transaction on apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, or Paytm, carefully read the notification. If you didn't initiate the transaction, decline the request immediately.
3.  **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Avoid using easily guessable passwords like your name or birthdate. Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for every academic and social media account.
4.  **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Activate 2FA on your email, GitHub, and LinkedIn profiles. This adds a second layer of security even if someone discovers your password.
5.  **Be Skeptical of "Urgent" Emails/Texts:** If you receive a message claiming your bank account is blocked or offering an "instant internship" that requires a fee, do not click any links. This is likely a phishing attempt.
6.  **Secure Your Social Media Privacy:** Set your profiles to "Private" or "Friends Only" to prevent strangers from gathering personal data that could be used for identity theft or social engineering.
7.  **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Sensitive Tasks:** Do not log into your bank accounts or college portals using free public Wi-Fi at cafes or stations. Use a secure mobile hotspot instead.
8.  **Keep Software Updated:** Regularly update your phone's OS and apps. These updates often include "security patches" that fix vulnerabilities exploited by hackers.

## 🚨 How to Report Cybercrime in India
If you or someone you know falls victim to a digital scam, report it immediately:

* **Official Portal:** [National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline:** **1930** (Available 24/7)
